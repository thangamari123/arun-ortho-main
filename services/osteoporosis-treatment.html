What We Treat

Understanding Osteoporosis

Osteoporosis is a systemic skeletal disease characterized by decreased bone density and deterioration of bone microarchitecture, leading to increased fragility and fracture risk. It is often called the 'silent disease' because bone loss occurs without symptoms until a fracture occurs.

At Arun Ortho, we offer comprehensive osteoporosis management including DEXA bone density scanning, metabolic workup, pharmacological treatment, fall prevention strategies, and surgical management of fragility fractures. Early intervention can dramatically reduce fracture risk.

Conditions

Conditions We Manage

??

Primary Osteoporosis

Age-related and postmenopausal bone loss requiring pharmacological treatment and lifestyle intervention.

??

Secondary Osteoporosis

Bone loss secondary to medications (steroids), endocrine disorders, or malabsorption requiring targeted treatment.

??

Fragility Fractures

Low-energy fractures of the wrist, hip, and spine indicating severe osteoporosis requiring urgent treatment.

??

Vertebral Compression Fractures

Painful collapse of spinal vertebrae due to osteoporosis, managed with bracing, medications, or vertebroplasty.

??

Osteopenia

Reduced bone density that has not yet reached osteoporosis threshold — early intervention is critical.

??

Paget's Disease of Bone

Focal disorder of bone remodelling causing bone deformity, pain, and increased fracture risk.

Treatment

Our Treatment Approach

01

DEXA Bone Density Scan

Gold-standard measurement of bone mineral density at hip and lumbar spine to diagnose and classify bone loss.

02

Laboratory Investigations

Blood and urine tests to identify secondary causes including calcium, vitamin D, thyroid, and parathyroid levels.

03

Medical Management

Bisphosphonates, denosumab, teriparatide, or hormone replacement therapy tailored to your risk profile.

04

Fracture Prevention

Fall prevention counselling, home safety assessment, calcium and vitamin D supplementation, and exercise guidance.

05

Lifestyle Counselling & Follow-up

Dietary advice, weight-bearing exercise program, smoking cessation, and regular DEXA monitoring every 1-2 years.
