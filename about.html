Our Story

About Arun Ortho Speciality Clinic

Dedicated to providing world-class orthopaedic care, restoring mobility, and improving the quality of life for all our patients.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded by Dr. Arun D., a renowned orthopaedic specialist, Arun Ortho Speciality Clinic aims to bridge the gap between advanced medical technology and compassionate patient care in Dharmapuri.

Our vision is to be the leading centre for orthopaedic excellence, offering minimally invasive procedures, joint replacements, and comprehensive rehabilitation under one roof. We believe in treating the patient, not just the disease, ensuring a swift and holistic recovery process.

  • ✔️ State-of-the-art diagnostic facilities
  • ✔️ Highly trained surgical and nursing staff
  • ✔️ Evidence-based rehabilitation protocols

Meet Dr. Arun D.

With over 10 years of intensive experience in joint replacements, trauma care, and arthroscopy, Dr. Arun brings unparalleled expertise to the region. His patient-first approach and dedication to modern surgical techniques ensure optimal outcomes with minimal recovery time.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your clinic timings? +

Our clinic is open Monday through Saturday from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM, and in the evening from 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM. Sundays are available by appointment only.

Do you accept health insurance? +

Yes, we accept a wide range of health insurance providers. Please bring your insurance card during your first visit, and our billing desk will assist you with the claims process.

Do I need a referral to see Dr. Arun? +

No referral is necessary to book an appointment with us. You can directly consult Dr. Arun for any orthopaedic concerns, whether it's acute pain, a sports injury, or a second opinion for surgery.

What should I bring to my first appointment? +

Please bring any past medical records, previous X-rays or MRI scans related to your condition, a list of your current medications, and a valid ID. This helps us accurately diagnose and formulate a treatment plan.
